Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Mix all dry ingredients together.
  3. Add milk and stir until smooth.
  4. Pour into a baking pan and bake for 30 minutes.
